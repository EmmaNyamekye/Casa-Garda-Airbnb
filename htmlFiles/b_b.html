Rooms

The first ensuite bedroom features a refreshing and calming green color scheme, which creates a peaceful and relaxing ambiance. The walls are painted in a soft green shade, and the bedding and curtains complement this color scheme perfectly. The room has two single beds, each with a comfortable mattress and fluffy pillows. The beds are positioned against opposite walls, providing guests with ample space and privacy. The room also includes a desk and chair, perfect for catching up on work or writing.

The second ensuite bedroom is elegantly designed with a soothing blue color palette, which creates a serene and tranquil atmosphere. The walls are painted in a pale blue hue, and the bedspread and curtains match this tone beautifully. The room has a luxurious king-size bed with plush pillows and a comfortable mattress, making it an ideal place to unwind after a long day. The room also has a seating area, where guests can sit and relax while enjoying the view. The ensuite bathroom is beautifully appointed, featuring a large bathtub and a separate shower, perfect for unwinding after a day of sightseeing.

Pets

The bed and breakfast exudes a warm and welcoming atmosphere towards pets of all kinds. The establishment prides itself on its pet-friendly attitude, making it an ideal destination for travelers who want to bring their furry friends along.

One of the standout features of the bed and breakfast is its stunning garden, which provides a peaceful and relaxing environment for guests and their pets to enjoy. With its well-maintained paths, colorful flowers, and lush greenery, the garden is a beautiful backdrop for a leisurely stroll or a game of fetch.

In addition to its pet-friendly policies and stunning garden, the bed and breakfast offers a tranquil and serene environment for guests to recharge and unwind. Whether guests want to read a book, sip a cup of coffee, or simply soak up the sun, the garden provides the perfect setting for any activity.
