Come and dip your feet in this wonderful lake

Activities

Lago di Garda is a stunning destination for those that love water sports, you can try windsurfing or sailing on the lake's crystal-clear waters.
If you prefer to stay on land, there are plenty of hiking and cycling trails that offer breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding mountains.
The area is also home to several ancient castles and villas.
